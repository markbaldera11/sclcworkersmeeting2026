Ministerial Vow

I am a Seventh-day Adventist Minister. Called to proclaim the Gospel of Salvation in Jesus Christ, the soon-coming Savior, and constrained by His eternal love, I vow to uphold my high calling. By God's grace, I shall live rightly, justly, with dignity, not exchanging this sacred ministry for any that is less noble, less enduring, less endearing. By His Spirit, I shall attain unto all excellence within reach. By His might I shall uphold His word. By His tender mercies, I shall be a true shepherd to my family, to my flock that all may know God's will and plan both in this world and in the world to come. Thus, I so commit and pledge all of myself to Him who called me out of darkness into His marvelous light. So help me God.
